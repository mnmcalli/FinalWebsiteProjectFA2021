PlayStation

Play has no limits.

PlayStation is a next-generation video game console company owned by Sony Interactive Entertainment. They have made a number of at-home gaming consoles, as well as a couple of portable devices. They also host a PlayStation Plus Subscription service which is necessary for playing online games with friends on the console. Sony was a pioneer of the gaming and DVD industry, and continues to sell DVD players as well as gaming consoles and accessories. They're a very diverse technology company at this point.

PlayStation offers next-generation consoles that rival other competitors like XBox. The relatively new PS5 has many new features compared to the older consoles, such as a newly refurbished user interface and special controllers that can be set to have different resistances for actions like pulling the trigger to a gun in Call of Duty. This console was released at the end of last year and continues to be a popular console. All consoles feature button map and UI customization to make every user comfortable with the layout.
